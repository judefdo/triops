Benefits

At Triops Info, we value the professional and personal well-being of our employees. Each employee we hire enjoys the following benefits:

Medical/Dental: Every employee’s needs are different, so we offer two medical and dental plans to accommodate individual preferences. Both plans cover preventive, basic and major services, but differ in how they pay for covered services.

Life Insurance: This benefit is an optional Group Term life insurance benefit.

Lifestyle Discounts: Employees receive discounts at selected fitness centers, hotels and car rental agencies.

401(k):We will soon offer a 401(k) plan and contribute a percentage of the employee’s salary to the plan.

Flexible Work Schedule: We believe that it’s easier to manage our personal and professional lives when we have some degree of flexibility in how, when and where we work.
